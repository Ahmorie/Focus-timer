# Focus Timer

## Product Purpose

A minimalist productivity timer application for deep work sessions. Helps users maintain focus through Pomodoro technique or custom countdown timers with elegant, distraction-free interface.

## Users

- Knowledge workers seeking focused work sessions
- Students studying for extended periods
- Professionals using Pomodoro technique
- Anyone needing timed focus blocks without distractions

## Register

**product** — This is a productivity tool where design serves the function. The timer is the core utility; aesthetics support usability and focus.

## Brand

**Luxurious minimalism meets futuristic precision**

- Black and white monochrome palette
- Glass morphism with subtle depth
- Ultra-clean typography with wide letter-spacing
- Smooth, purposeful animations
- Premium feel without decoration

## Anti-References

- ❌ Colorful, playful productivity apps (Habitica, Forest)
- ❌ Busy, feature-packed interfaces (Toggl Track)
- ❌ Gamified experiences with badges/rewards
- ❌ Cluttered dashboards with statistics
- ❌ Bright, high-contrast color schemes

## Strategic Principles

1. **Singular focus**: Timer is always center-stage
2. **Calm interface**: No visual noise or distractions
3. **Immediate usability**: Zero learning curve
4. **Persistent precision**: Timer accuracy even in background
5. **Sensory feedback**: Subtle sound and title updates

## Physical Scene

A developer in a dim home office at night, laptop screen the only light source, needing to track a deep work session without pulling attention away from code. The timer should feel like a companion, not an interruption.

**Result**: Dark theme with subtle contrast, large readable numbers, minimal cognitive load.
